Hi, I'm Raima Saha

Developer / Designer / Artist

I always look for ways to interpret the things and places around me differently. A lot of my art reflects my tendency to revisit things in my mind, just like I do in my daily life. Sometimes, revisiting the various aspects of my life through my art allows me to learn and absorb more meaning from it. I can analyze interactions, moments, and facets of my life in ways that I might not be able to otherwise, such as with color, nuance, and emotion. It is introspection through creation, something that allows me solace as I continue my journey as a human and artist.

As someone who is perfectionistic with everything she does, art, somehow, allows me to decompress and channel that energy into something...productive, is it? Something of value that could potentially evoke something similar or something completely different from the viewer.

I bring this same approach with all my work. Everything I do, make, create, and build toes the boundaries of bold and beautiful. Explore some of my work.
